Our Story

Every periodontist knows the frustration: you spend 10-15 minutes at each recall explaining the same brushing techniques, the same interdental cleaning routine, the same dietary advice. Your patients nod along, leave the chair—and forget everything by the time they get home.

Then they return months later with disease progression, and you start all over again.

We built Perioskoup to break this cycle. Our AI dental companion keeps patients on track between visits with personalized guidance, smart reminders, and progress tracking. When patients return, you can focus on actual treatment—not re-education.

In 2025, Perioskoup was awarded 3rd Prize at the EFP Digital Innovation Award at EuroPerio11 in Vienna. We're humbled by the recognition from the European Federation of Periodontology—and motivated to keep building.
