Frequently Asked Questions

AI Dental Companion FAQ

Common questions about our dental companion app and how it helps dental practices.

What is an AI dental companion?

An AI dental companion is a digital health tool that uses artificial intelligence to support both patients and clinicians in managing oral health. Perioskoup's dental companion app provides personalized guidance, smart reminders, and tracks patient progress to improve adherence and clinical outcomes between visits.

How much time does Perioskoup save dentists?

Dentists using our AI dental companion report saving 10-15 minutes per high-risk patient visit. The dental companion app handles patient education between visits, reducing repetitive hygiene coaching and freeing up valuable chair time.

Is Perioskoup GDPR compliant?

Yes, our AI dental companion is fully GDPR compliant with end-to-end encryption. All patient data in the dental companion app is secure and protected according to European and UK data protection standards.

Does the dental companion app reduce patient no-shows?

Yes, Perioskoup significantly reduces patient no-shows through smart reminders and continuous engagement. Our AI dental companion keeps patients connected to their practice between visits, improving appointment adherence.

What platforms does the AI dental companion support?

Perioskoup is available on iOS, Android, and web platforms. Our dental companion app works seamlessly across all devices, allowing patients to track their oral health anywhere and anytime.

How do I get started with Perioskoup?

Getting started is easy. Sign up for early access on our website, and our team will help you onboard your practice onto the AI dental companion platform within days. It's free for early adopters with no credit card required.
