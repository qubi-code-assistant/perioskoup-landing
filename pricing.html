Pricing FAQ

Is there a free trial?
Yes! During beta, founding clinics get 3 months completely free. No credit card required. After launch, we'll offer a 14-day free trial.
Are there any per-patient fees?
No. The Clinic Plan includes unlimited patients. The optional Patient Pro upgrade (€6/month) is paid by patients who want premium features—not by the clinic.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), SEPA direct debit for EU clinics, and bank transfer for annual plans.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
Do you offer discounts for multiple locations?
Yes! Contact us for multi-location pricing. We offer volume discounts starting at 3+ locations.
What happens to my patients' data if I cancel?
You can export all data before cancellation. After 30 days, data is securely deleted per GDPR requirements. Patients keep access to their own app data.
